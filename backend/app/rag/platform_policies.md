# Platform Advertising Policies

## google
Platform: Google Performance Max

### Creative Constraints
- Headlines: max 15 items, each max 30 characters
- Descriptions: max 4 items, each max 90 characters
- Long headlines: max 5 items, each max 90 characters
- Images: at least one required; accepted ratios 1:1 (square) and 1.91:1 (landscape)
- Videos: optional, but recommended for Performance Max
- Final URLs: required, must be a valid landing page URL

### Bidding & Campaign
- Bidding strategy: Maximize conversion value (required for Performance Max)
- Campaign type: PERFORMANCE_MAX
- Daily budget set at campaign level

### Targeting
- Audience signals accepted (not hard targeting — used as hints)
- Geo and language targeting supported
- Keywords used as audience signals, not direct keyword targeting

### Approval Rules
- No misleading claims
- No prohibited content (weapons, adult, etc.)
- Brand safety guidelines apply

## meta
Platform: Meta Shopping / Catalog Sales

### Creative Constraints
- Primary text: max 125 characters
- Headline: max 40 characters
- Description (link): max 30 characters
- Image aspect ratio: 1:1 (square) or 1.91:1 (landscape)
- Video: 1:1 or 4:5 ratio recommended
- Catalog required for Shopping / Catalog Sales objective

### Bidding & Campaign
- Campaign objective: OUTCOME_SALES
- Bidding: Lowest cost (default) or cost cap
- Ad set daily budget in cents (multiply dollars × 100)
- Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns supported

### Targeting
- Geo: country-level targeting via geo_locations.countries
- Broad targeting recommended for Advantage+ campaigns
- Custom audiences and lookalikes supported
- Age minimum: 18

### Approval Rules
- No before/after imagery implying transformation
- No discrimination in targeting
- Financial products require special ad category declaration

## amazon
Platform: Amazon Sponsored Brands

### Creative Constraints
- Brand name: max 30 characters
- Headline: max 50 characters
- Logo image: required (at least 400×400 px, PNG or JPEG)
- Custom image: optional, recommended for higher CTR
- Landing page: brand store or product list page

### Bidding & Campaign
- Campaign type: sponsoredBrands
- Bidding strategy: dynamic-bids-down-only (legacyForSales) recommended for new campaigns
- Daily budget minimum: $1.00
- Start date: must be today or future date (ISO 8601)

### Targeting
- Keyword match types: exact, phrase, broad
- Negative keywords supported
- Product targeting (ASIN/category) supported
- Bid per keyword recommended: $0.50–$2.00 for new campaigns

### Approval Rules
- Brand must be enrolled in Amazon Brand Registry
- No competitor brand names in headline
- Headline must be relevant to the advertised products
